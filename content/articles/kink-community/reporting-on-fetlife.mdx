FetLife is a community, and communities are safer when members report bad behavior. If you've witnessed or experienced harassment, abuse, or consent violations, here's how to report it effectively.

---

## What Can Be Reported

### Terms of Service Violations

**FetLife prohibits:**
- Harassment and stalking
- Non-consensual content (images, videos)
- Solicitation of minors
- Threats and intimidation
- Spam and scam accounts
- Impersonation
- Illegal content

### Consent Violations

**Community safety issues:**
- Reports of sexual assault or abuse
- Consent violations during play
- Predatory behavior patterns
- Boundary violations

### Important Distinction

**FetLife is not:**
- A court of law
- Equipped to investigate crimes
- A replacement for police reports when appropriate
- Always able to act on he-said/she-said situations

**FetLife can:**
- Remove accounts that violate terms
- Restrict access for bad actors
- Take action on clear violations
- Support community safety within limits

---

## How to Report to FetLife

### In-App Reporting

**For profiles:**
1. Go to the user's profile
2. Click the three-dot menu (options)
3. Select "Report"
4. Choose the type of violation
5. Provide details
6. Submit

**For content (posts, photos, etc.):**
1. Find the specific content
2. Click the report option
3. Select violation type
4. Provide context
5. Submit

### Direct Contact

**For serious issues:**
- Email: caretakers@fetlife.com
- Use for complex situations
- Provide detailed information
- Include evidence if available

### What to Include

**Good report includes:**
- Specific behaviors or violations
- Dates and context when possible
- Screenshots (if relevant)
- Links to problematic content
- Impact on you or others
- Any witnesses

## Reporting Harassment

### Types of Harassment

**What qualifies:**
- Repeated unwanted contact after being told to stop
- Threatening messages
- Public attacks or defamation
- Stalking behavior
- Creating hostile environment

### Documenting Harassment

**Before reporting:**
- Screenshot messages (with timestamps)
- Note dates and patterns
- Save any relevant evidence
- Document witnesses if applicable

### Steps to Take

**Recommended order:**
1. Block the person (stops immediate contact)
2. Screenshot evidence
3. Report to FetLife
4. Inform community leaders if relevant
5. Consider police report if threats are serious

## Reporting Consent Violations

### Sensitive Territory

**Important notes:**
- FetLife takes consent seriously
- They cannot investigate like law enforcement
- Your safety is the priority
- Multiple reports strengthen cases

### What Helps

**Effective reports include:**
- Specific description of what happened
- When and where
- Any witnesses
- Pattern of behavior (if known)
- Other reports you're aware of

### Realistic Expectations

**FetLife may:**
- Investigate the account
- Remove or restrict the user
- Take action based on terms violations
- Not provide detailed feedback on outcome

**FetLife typically cannot:**
- Conduct full investigations
- Make determinations in ambiguous cases
- Replace legal processes
- Guarantee specific outcomes

## Community Reporting

### Beyond FetLife Platform

**Community options:**
- Local group moderators
- Event organizers
- Community leaders
- Consent boards (where they exist)

### When to Use Community Channels

**In addition to FetLife:**
- When someone poses ongoing risk to local community
- When event organizers need to know
- When pattern of behavior affects multiple people
- When you want community accountability

### How Community Reports Work

**Typical process:**
- Report to event organizers or group admins
- They may have their own investigation process
- Possible outcomes: warnings, bans, community notification
- Varies by community

## What Happens After Reporting

### FetLife's Process

**Typical flow:**
- Report received and reviewed
- May request additional information
- Investigation as warranted
- Action taken (or not)
- Often no detailed feedback to reporter

### Possible Outcomes

**Actions FetLife might take:**
- Warning to the user
- Content removal
- Account restrictions
- Account deletion
- No action (if insufficient evidence)

### Timeline

**Expect:**
- Acknowledgment of report (sometimes)
- Investigation time varies
- May hear nothing about resolution
- Serious issues may be faster

## Protecting Yourself

### Before Issues Arise

**Prevention:**
- Vet people before meeting
- Use FetLife's privacy features
- Trust your instincts
- Build community connections for references

### If You're Experiencing Abuse

**Immediate safety:**
- Block the person
- Remove yourself from dangerous situations
- Reach out to trusted friends
- Consider professional support

### Emotional Aftermath

**Taking care:**
- Reporting can be emotionally taxing
- You don't have to report if it's too much
- Seek support from friends or professionals
- Your wellbeing comes first

## When to Involve Authorities

### Police Reports

**Consider involving police for:**
- Physical assault
- Sexual assault
- Credible threats of violence
- Stalking that extends offline
- Any crime

### FetLife Isn't Enough

**Legal action may be needed for:**
- Serious crimes
- Ongoing danger
- When you need legal protection (restraining orders)
- Evidence that should be preserved legally

### Documentation for Legal

**If involving police:**
- Preserve all digital evidence
- Don't delete anything
- Note dates, times, witnesses
- Consider consulting an attorney

## False Reports and Responsibility

### Don't Report Falsely

**Important:**
- False reports are taken seriously
- Weaponizing reports damages trust
- Report genuine violations only
- Don't report people because you're angry at them

### What's Not Reportable

**Not typically violations:**
- People you don't like
- Consensual activities you find distasteful
- Disagreements or arguments
- Someone saying no to you
- Negative (truthful) things someone said about you

## Supporting Others

### If Someone Reports to You

**Be supportive:**
- Listen without judgment
- Believe their experience
- Ask how you can help
- Offer to help them report if they want
- Respect their choices about reporting

### Witness to Problems

**If you see something:**
- You can report as a witness
- Offer support to affected person
- Don't share details without consent
- Consider community safety

## FAQ

**Will the person know I reported them?**
FetLife typically keeps reporter identity confidential, but absolute anonymity isn't guaranteed. In community reports, your identity may be known.

**What if nothing happens after my report?**
FetLife may not act on all reports, especially in ambiguous situations. You've done what you can. Consider community channels if appropriate.

**Can I report something that happened offline?**
Yes, if the person is on FetLife. Especially if there's a pattern or ongoing risk. FetLife cares about community safety broadly.

**What if I'm falsely reported?**
FetLife will review the situation. If you didn't violate terms, you should be fine. Consider whether there's a conflict that needs addressing.

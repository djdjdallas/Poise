Feeld works on both free and paid tiers—but what do you actually get at each level? Understanding the real differences helps you decide whether Majestic is worth it for you.

Here's the complete comparison.

---

## What's Free on Feeld

### Core Features

**Free includes:**
- Create profile and add photos
- Set preferences and desires
- Basic matching (swipe, match, message)
- Messaging with matches
- Link with a partner
- Join groups

### Swiping and Matching

**Free limitations:**
- Daily like limit
- Standard discovery queue
- Can't see who liked you before matching
- Basic matching algorithm

### Filters and Discovery

**Free filters:**
- Basic filters (age, distance, gender)
- Can't filter by desires
- Limited location control
- Standard discovery

### Messaging

**Free messaging:**
- Unlimited messages to matches
- No read receipts
- Standard messaging features
- Can send photos in chat

## What Majestic Adds

### Seeing Interest

## FAQ

**Can I find connections on free Feeld?**
Absolutely. Free Feeld works—premium just adds efficiency. Many people find great matches without paying.

**What's the most valuable premium feature?**
For most users: seeing who liked you. It eliminates guessing and saves significant time.

**If I cancel Majestic, do I lose matches?**
No. Your matches and conversations stay. You just lose access to premium features.

**Do paid users get shown more?**
Feeld doesn't officially say, but premium features make you more visible through things like incognito control and can change results through more strategic use.

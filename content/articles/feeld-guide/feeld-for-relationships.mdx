## Profile Setup for Relationships

### Signal What You Want

**Make it clear:**
- State you're seeking meaningful connection
- Describe relationship style you want
- Be specific about what you're looking for
- Show depth and substance

**Examples:**
- "Looking for connection that could grow into something real"
- "Seeking partners, not just playmates"
- "ENM and looking for another genuine relationship"

### Show Relationship-Ready Qualities

**Your profile should convey:**
- Emotional availability
- What you bring to relationships
- Your values and priorities
- Depth of personality

### Photos for Relationships

**Choose photos that:**
- Show your personality
- Convey warmth and approachability
- Include activities and interests
- Suggest someone worth getting to know

## FAQ

**Is Feeld good for finding serious relationships?**
Yes—especially for open-minded, ENM, or non-traditional relationships. Be clear about what you want.

**How do I signal I want a relationship, not just hookups?**
State it directly in profile and confirm in early messages. Show depth and substance.

**What if everyone seems to want casual?**
Filter better, be patient, and be very clear about your goals. Right people are there.

**Should I use Feeld or a traditional dating app for relationships?**
Depends what kind of relationship. Feeld is great for ENM, kink-aware, or unconventional. Traditional apps for mainstream relationships.

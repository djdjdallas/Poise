Here's how to use Feeld for friendship.

## Profile Setup for Friendship

### Being Clear

**State in your profile:**
- You're open to friendship
- What kind of friends you're seeking
- That it doesn't have to be romantic/sexual
- Why you're seeking community

**Examples:**
- "Also happy to make friends who understand ENM life"
- "Looking for community as much as connection"
- "Open to genuine friendship with like-minded people"

### What to Include

**Show:**
- Your interests and hobbies
- What you're about beyond dating
- Shared activities you'd enjoy
- Your community values

## FAQ

**Is it weird to use Feeld for friendship?**
Not at all. Many people seek community alongside (or instead of) dating. Be clear about your intentions.

**What if they think I want to date?**
Be explicit from the start that you're seeking friendship. Reiterate if needed. Clear communication prevents misunderstanding.

**Can friendships become romantic later?**
Sometimes—if both people want that. But don't approach friendship as backdoor to dating.

**Where else can I find kink/ENM friends?**
Munches, FetLife events, poly meetups, community gatherings. Feeld is one avenue among many.

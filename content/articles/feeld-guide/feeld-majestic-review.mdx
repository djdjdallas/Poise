## Feature-by-Feature Analysis

### See Who Likes You

**Value:** High
**Why:** The biggest time-saver. Instead of swiping hoping for matches, you see who's already interested.

**Best for:** Anyone who wants efficient matching

### Advanced Filters

**Value:** Medium-High
**Why:** Filters out incompatible people before you see them. Saves swipes and time.

**Best for:** People with specific preferences

### Unlimited Likes

**Value:** Medium
**Why:** Removes the artificial limit, but more likes doesn't mean more quality matches.

**Best for:** Heavy users who hit limits

### Location Change

**Value:** High for some, low for others
**Why:** Essential if traveling or in small markets. Useless if you don't need it.

**Best for:** Travelers, people in smaller areas

### Incognito Mode

**Value:** High for privacy-focused
**Why:** Browse without appearing to others. Control who sees you.

**Best for:** Those with privacy needs

### Read Receipts

**Value:** Low-Medium
**Why:** Nice to know, but can create anxiety. Mixed benefit.

**Best for:** Those who won't overthink it

## FAQ

**Can I try Majestic before committing?**
Feeld occasionally offers trials or discounts. Watch for promotions before subscribing long-term.

**Will I lose my data if I unsubscribe?**
Your matches and conversations remain. You just lose access to premium features.

**Does Majestic guarantee more matches?**
No—it helps you find interested people faster, but your profile determines your matches.

**Should I get the longest subscription for best rate?**
Only if you're confident you'll use it that long. Shorter can be safer to start.
